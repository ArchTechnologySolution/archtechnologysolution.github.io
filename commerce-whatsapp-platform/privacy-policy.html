Privacy Policy

Commerce WhatsApp Platform

Last Updated: December 29, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Commerce WhatsApp Platform ("Platform", "App", "we", "our", or "us"), developed and operated by Arch Technology Solution. We are a technology provider for Meta Business Suite, specializing in automating order management processes and integrating WhatsApp messaging capabilities for businesses.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your information when you use our Commerce WhatsApp Platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in our data practices.

Important Notice: This platform integrates with Meta Business Suite, WhatsApp Business API, and uses third-party services including Medusa, Supabase, and Firebase. We process business and customer data to provide order management and messaging automation services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Business Information

When you register your business on our platform, we collect:

  • Business name and registration details
  • Contact information (email, phone number, business address)
  • Tax identification numbers and business licenses
  • WhatsApp Business Account credentials and API access tokens
  • Meta Business Suite account information
  • Payment and billing information

2.2 Customer Information

Through your use of the platform for order management, we may process:

  • Customer names and contact details (phone numbers, WhatsApp numbers)
  • Order details, product preferences, and purchase history
  • Delivery addresses and shipping information
  • Payment transaction data
  • Communication history and message content sent via WhatsApp

2.3 Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • IP addresses and device identifiers
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system and platform
  • Usage data and interaction with the platform
  • Log files and analytics data
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2.4 WhatsApp Message Data

When you use our WhatsApp integration features:

  • Message content sent and received through WhatsApp Business API
  • Message metadata (timestamps, delivery status, read receipts)
  • Media files shared in conversations (images, documents)
  • Customer responses and interaction patterns

3.1 Platform Services

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Commerce WhatsApp Platform
  • Process and manage orders through automated workflows
  • Send order confirmations, updates, and notifications via WhatsApp
  • Facilitate communication between businesses and their customers
  • Integrate with Meta Business Suite for business management
  • Process payments and manage billing

3.2 Platform Improvement

We analyze data to:

  • Improve platform functionality and user experience
  • Develop new features and services
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Debug technical issues and optimize performance
  • Conduct research and analytics

3.3 Communication

We use your information to:

  • Send administrative messages and platform updates
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Notify you about changes to our services or policies

3.4 Legal and Security

We process data to:

  • Comply with legal obligations and regulations
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Protect the rights, property, and safety of our users
  • Enforce our terms of service and policies

4. Third-Party Services and Integrations

4.1 Meta Business Suite & WhatsApp Business API

Our platform integrates with Meta Business Suite and WhatsApp Business API. Data shared through WhatsApp is subject to Meta's Privacy Policy and WhatsApp's Terms of Service. We act as a technology provider facilitating this integration.

4.2 Backend Infrastructure

We use the following third-party services to operate our platform:

  • Medusa: E-commerce backend for order management and product catalog
  • Supabase: Database and authentication services for secure data storage
  • Firebase: Real-time database, authentication, and admin panel functionality

4.3 Data Sharing with Third Parties

We share data with third parties only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted vendors who help us operate the platform (Medusa, Supabase, Firebase, hosting providers)
  • Meta/WhatsApp: When sending messages through WhatsApp Business API
  • Payment Processors: For processing transactions securely
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • With Consent: When you explicitly consent to data sharing

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard cloud infrastructure through Supabase and Firebase. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information.

5.2 Security Measures

We implement various security measures, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Business data is retained while your account is active. Customer data is retained according to your business requirements and applicable regulations.
