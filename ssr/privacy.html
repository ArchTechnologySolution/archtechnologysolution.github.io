Privacy Policy

Stamp Stock Register

Last Updated: December 6, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Stamp Stock Register ("App", "we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a transparent understanding of how our application operates. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, usage, and protection.

Important Notice: Stamp Stock Register is designed with your privacy as a top priority. The App operates entirely offline, does not collect any personal data, and does not transmit any information over the internet.

2. Data Collection

2.1 Personal Information

We do NOT collect, store, transmit, or process any personal information. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Contacts or photos from your device
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Any other personally identifiable information (PII)

2.2 Application Data

All data you enter into Stamp Stock Register (such as stamp inventory records, transactions, and other related information) is stored locally on your device. This data:

  • Remains exclusively on your device
  • Is never transmitted to our servers or any third-party services
  • Is accessible only to you
  • Is not backed up to cloud services by our App

3. Internet Connectivity

Stamp Stock Register operates completely offline and does not require internet connectivity for its core functionality. The App:

  • Does not connect to the internet
  • Does not send or receive data over the network
  • Does not communicate with external servers
  • Does not integrate with online services or APIs
Exception: Internet connectivity is only used for processing in-app purchases through Google Play's secure payment system. This connection is handled entirely by Google Play and is not controlled by our App.

4. In-App Purchases

Stamp Stock Register offers optional in-app purchases to unlock premium features. When you make an in-app purchase:

  • The transaction is processed securely by Google Play
  • We do not have access to your payment information
  • We do not store credit card or payment details
  • Google Play handles all billing information according to their privacy policy
  • Purchase records are maintained by Google Play, not by our App

For information about how Google Play handles your payment data, please refer to the Google Privacy Policy.

5. Permissions

Stamp Stock Register requires minimal permissions to function. Any permissions requested are used solely for the App's local functionality and do not involve data transmission. The App may request:

  • Storage: To save and retrieve your stamp inventory data locally on your device
  • Billing (In-app purchases): To process optional premium feature purchases through Google Play

We do not request or use any other permissions that could compromise your privacy, such as camera, microphone, contacts, or location access (unless specifically required for App functionality and clearly disclosed).

6. Data Security

Since all your data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted, the security of your data depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Using a secure lock screen on your device
  • Keeping your device's operating system up to date
  • Regularly backing up your device data to prevent data loss
  • Being cautious when granting app permissions

We implement industry-standard security practices in our App's code to protect against unauthorized access to locally stored data.

7. Third-Party Services

Stamp Stock Register does not integrate with or use any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. The only external service interaction is with Google Play for in-app purchase processing, which is governed by Google's privacy policy.

8. Children's Privacy

Our App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected or transmitted, the App is safe for use by individuals of all ages. However, in-app purchases should be supervised by parents or guardians.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You have complete control over your data
  • You can delete data at any time through the App's interface or by uninstalling the App
  • Uninstalling the App will permanently remove all locally stored data
  • We do not retain any copies of your data on our servers (because we don't collect any)

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Significant changes will be communicated through the App or App store listing
  • Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

11. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, traditional data privacy rights (such as access, correction, deletion, or data portability) do not apply in the conventional sense. However:

  • You have complete ownership of all data stored on your device
  • You can export, backup, or delete your data at any time
  • You have full control over the App's permissions on your device

12. Legal Compliance

Our App is designed to comply with major privacy regulations including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • Other applicable data protection laws

Compliance is straightforward as we do not collect, process, or store any personal data.
